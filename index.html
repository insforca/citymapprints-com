Everything you need to know

A city map print is a poster or art print showing the street layout of a specific city or neighbourhood. They range from pre-made catalog prints to fully custom designs where you choose the exact location, zoom, colour palette, and sometimes the map rotation angle. Custom prints cost $25–$80 unframed.
Yes — if you use a custom map service. MapVibe Studio, Mapiful, and Craft & Oak all support any city worldwide using real OpenStreetMap data. Catalog services like Native Maps or GeoJango are limited to ~50–200 US cities only.
A catalog map print is pre-designed — you pick from existing options and can't change the location. A custom map print lets you set the exact centre point, zoom to your street, choose colours, and sometimes control the map rotation angle. Custom prints are better for gifts; catalog prints have more polished editorial aesthetics for popular cities.
