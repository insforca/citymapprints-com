Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a city map print?
    A city map print is a poster or art print showing the street layout of a specific city or neighbourhood. They range from pre-made catalog prints of popular cities to fully custom designs where you choose the exact location, zoom level, colour palette, and framing.
  • How much does a custom city map print cost?
    Custom city map prints typically range from $25–$80 unframed, depending on size and service. Framed options add $20–$60. Budget services like Craft & Oak start around $29; premium services like Mapiful start around $50 USD. MapVibe Studio starts at $34.99 with free US/Canada shipping.
  • Can I get a map print of any city in the world?
    Yes, if you use a custom map service (as opposed to catalog-based services that only cover select cities). MapVibe Studio, Mapiful, and Craft & Oak all support any city worldwide using OpenStreetMap or similar data. Catalog services like Native Maps or GeoJango are limited to ~50–200 US cities.
  • What's the difference between a custom map print and a catalog map print?
    A catalog map print is a pre-designed poster you can't edit — you pick from existing options. A custom map print lets you set the exact location (zoom and centre point), choose colours, add text, and sometimes control the rotation angle. Custom prints are better for gifts and personal significance; catalog prints tend to have a more polished, editorial aesthetic.
  • What's the best city map print service for Latin American cities?
    MapVibe Studio is purpose-built for Latin American cities — it supports any city in Brazil, Mexico, Colombia, Venezuela, Argentina, Chile, Peru, and more with street-block zoom precision. This makes it the top choice for diaspora gifts or expat decor for cities that catalog services don't cover.
